Job Market Paper

The Role of Energy Efficiency in Productivity: Evidence from Canada
Quantifies productivity losses from misallocated capital, labor, and energy across Canadian sectors using a province-sector framework. Results show that inefficient energy use alone explains up to 2.5% of the total gap.
